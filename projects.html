Openbook

I completed this project for my SAT in VCE Applied Computing: Software Development when I accelerated the subject in 2024. The concept is a free online platform for students, teachers, tutors and academics to access purpose-designed textbooks for VCE. All textbooks are made available under an open license, meaning that they can be updated and collaborated on by the community to ensure up-to-date content (I built an accompanying textbook templating CLI tool for this).
